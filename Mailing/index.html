İmbat in 2024

Top 6 Highlights of the year:

As we conclude 2024, we are thrilled to share the accomplishments that have marked this year as a significant milestone in Imbat's 34-year journey. These achievements demonstrate our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and global expansion as we prepare for an even brighter 2025.

Expanding Horizons with Quality Standards:

This year, as İmbat, we proudly introduced Eurovent certified solutions to new markets, including Norway, Estonia, Hungary, Belgium, Albania, and Cuba. Meanwhile, our team members on the Eurovent Board actively contribute to advancing HVAC industry standards and shaping its future vision, reinforcing İmbat’s role as a trusted player worldwide.

Advancing Digital Transformation:

In 2024, we accelerated our digital transformation journey, assessed our digital maturity level, and enhanced our digital processes to improve efficiency. These efforts enabled us to deliver faster and more responsive customer solutions, strengthening our ability to meet their needs effectively.

Product Innovations — Meet Perseus:

Our new Perseus Rooftop Series, with its modular design, energy efficiency, and eco-friendly R454B refrigerant, is a testament to our commitment to sustainable HVAC technology. Receiving strong interest at its launch, Perseus is designed as a high performance, adaptable solution for today’s demands in energy-efficient products

Social Responsibility Initiatives:

In 2024, İmbat continued its dedication to social responsibility, supporting projects in education and environmental sustainability. Through initiatives like “Young Women Shaping Their Future,” we empowered women economically and socially. We strengthened collaborations with İzmir Institute of Technology and Ege University and contributed actively to our industry through partnerships with İSKİD, ESSİAD, MUKAD, and TTMD.

R&D and Product Development:

Our R&D efforts brought about exciting new products in 2024, including the Sirius Micro channel Condenser Unit Series, the Perseus Rooftop Series, and the Orion Portable Rooftop Unit Series. Additionally, this year's innovations enhanced our passenger bridge air conditioners, aircraft cooling systems, and data center cooling solutions, all showcasing İmbat's unwavering commitment to continuous improvement and the delivery of cost effective HVAC solutions.

Global Presence at Events and Exhibitions:

We proudly represented Turkey at international HVAC events, including MCE Milano and Chillventa, where we focused on building and strengthening partnerships. By presenting our innovative products and artistic concepts, we successfully entered new markets, including Chile, Uruguay, Argentina, Portugal, Azerbaijan, and Iraq, further broadening İmbat's global network and reach.

Learn More About İmbat:

With these achievements, İmbat has continued to make a difference in the HVAC industry in 2024. We look forward to driving innovation and expanding our impact in 2025. If you'd like to connect or learn more about our recent accomplishments, please feel free to reach out.
